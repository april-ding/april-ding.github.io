Shanhai 山海

Skills

3D Environment Modeling / Motion Graphics

Overview

"Shanhai" in Chinese means "Mountain Sea". The environment and creature designs are inspired by the mythic geography and beasts from the Chinese book "Classic of Mountains and Seas". The video follows a flying fish and presents a non-linear story through its eyes. This is meant to be a nonsensical meditative experience that puts the audience in a mythical world.

Featured in UCLA Design Media Arts Undergraduate 2019 Exhibition "Migration"

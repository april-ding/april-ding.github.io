3-Wall Video Projection installed in UCLA Broad Art Center EDA | Cinema 4D, Adobe After Effects

This piece is a result of careful considerations of audio, world-building, and camera perspectives. I aim to construct an experience that purifying to the mind by presenting an imaginary world filled with strange plants and creatures.

I incorporated smooth motion inspired by natural behaviors, such as flowers blooming, leaves swaying in the wind, animals swimming. In a future where humans have exhausted Earth's natural resources, would the only places to see these natural spectacles be these artificially constructed virtual realities?
